About the Band

Four Musicians.
One Unstoppable
Dance Floor.

The Barnhouse Band is an energetic four-piece based in the South of England, built from years of professional experience across studios, stages, and some of the UK's biggest venues.

We bring a diverse repertoire spanning The Beatles to Harry Styles, Fleetwood Mac to Daft Punk - tailored to fill dance floors at weddings, parties, and events of every size. From acoustic duos for intimate afternoon sets to the full four-piece with horns for a packed evening, we shape our performance to your vision.

We travel nationwide and take pride in making every event feel personal, polished, and absolutely unforgettable.

Meet the Band

The People Behind the Sound

Ryan Butterworth

Lead Vocals & Guitar

Professionally trained vocalist with acclaim from BBC Music and national press. Reached the live finals of BBC One's 'Let It Shine'. A prolific songwriter with exceptional stage presence.

Jamie White

Lead Guitar

One of the UK's top session guitarists with 100+ records to his name. Has recorded at Abbey Road and performed across North America and Europe.

Brett Neville

Bass Guitar

Versatile freelance musician with extensive touring history across the UK and Europe. A respected figure in the jazz scene who brings groove and depth to every performance.

Jamie Smith

Drums

Highly sought-after session drummer. Has collaborated with musicians associated with Paul McCartney, Roger Daltrey, Jethro Tull, Portishead, and Cliff Richard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How much does it cost to hire the band?

Packages start from £225 for a solo acoustic performance, up to £1,600+ for the full four-piece band. A 6-piece option with horns is also available. Every event is different, so get in touch for a tailored quote.

Do you travel outside of Dorset?

Yes - we're based in Dorset but regularly perform at weddings and events across the entire UK. Travel costs may apply for distant venues.

Can you learn our first dance song?

Absolutely. We love learning first dance songs and are happy to rehearse yours so it's performed live on the night. Just let us know your choice when you book.

Is a DJ included?

Yes - a DJ service comes included as standard with every booking, keeping the music going between live sets so your dance floor never empties.

What kind of events do you play?

Weddings are our speciality, but we also perform at corporate events, private parties, birthday celebrations, charity galas, and festivals.

How long do your sets last?

We typically perform either 2×60 or 3×40 minute live sets. We're flexible and happy to discuss timings that suit your event schedule.
