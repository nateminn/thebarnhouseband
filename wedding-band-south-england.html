Wedding Band Across South England

Based in Dorset with nationwide reach, The Barnhouse Band is a go-to choice for weddings and events across Southern England — from Cornwall to Kent, Devon to London.

Nationwide Coverage, Southern Roots

While we're proudly based in Dorset, we regularly travel across the South of England for weddings and events. Our reach extends from the West Country through to London and the Home Counties, covering Devon, Somerset, Wiltshire, Hampshire, Surrey, Sussex, Kent, and everywhere in between.

For venues within approximately two hours of our Dorset base, travel is typically included or carries a minimal surcharge. For longer distances, we'll provide a transparent quote upfront — no hidden costs.

Event Types

Weddings are our core business, but we perform at all types of events across Southern England. Corporate functions, charity galas, milestone birthdays, festival stages, and private parties all benefit from professional live music that reads the room and delivers the right energy.

Areas We Regularly Cover

Our most popular areas for bookings include Dorset and Bournemouth, Hampshire and Southampton, Wiltshire and Salisbury, Devon and Exeter, Somerset and Bath, Surrey and Sussex, and Greater London. Wherever your event is in the South of England, we'll make it work.

How to Book

Check our availability by getting in touch with your date, venue, and preferred package. We'll respond within 24 hours with pricing tailored to your event, including any applicable travel costs.
